ABOUT US

Anabel Egner (she/her) and Philipp Eckel (he/him) met while studying at KIT Karlsruhe. After five years working in renowned offices in Berlin, they decided to move to Santiago de Chile. There, they founded PHILYBEL, a collaborative practice that explores the production of architecture through diverse approaches — always seeking reinvention and refusing to be limited by style or dogma.
Playful yet precise. Sustainable but never greenwashed. Every line matters. Slick yet raw. Talk less, make more. Every project could be the last — so make it count.
